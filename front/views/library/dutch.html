Dutch Defense

ECO Code: A81

Opening Moves

1. d4 f5

Overview

The Dutch Defense traditionally begins 1.d4 f5. Black sometimes plays 1...e6 followed by 2...f5 in order to avoid the Staunton Gambit.

Strengths

  • Original
  • Creates imblanced yet flexible positions
  • Less common, meaning opponents may not be as prepared to play it

Weaknesses

  • Early ...f5 exposes Black king
  • While less common, an opponent prepared may prove difficult
  • White gains central space
