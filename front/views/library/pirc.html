Pirc Defense

ECO Code: B07-B09

Opening Moves

1. e4 d6 2. d4 Nf6

Overview

The Pirc Defense is a complicated hypermodern reply to the King's Pawn Opening. Black's idea with 1...d6 is to follow 2...Nf6, without giving White the option to kick the knight by advancing with e5.

Strengths

  • Black has a lot of freedom of different ways to play
  • Tricky, active defesne
  • White can be provoked easily

Weaknesses

  • White has several systems which lead to a raging attack
  • White can play carefully to restrict Black
  • White has many differnt ways to combat the Pirc
