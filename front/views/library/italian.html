Italian Game

ECO Code: C50–C54

Opening Moves

1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. Bc4

Overview

The Italian Game is one of the oldest recorded chess openings, emphasizing quick development and control of the center. It leads to open positions and can transition into multiple variations like the Giuoco Piano or Evans Gambit.

Strengths

  • Easy for beginners to learn
  • Strong central control
  • Flexible development paths

Weaknesses

  • Can lead to passive positions if not played actively
  • Sharp tactics in certain lines
