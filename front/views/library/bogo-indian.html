Bogo-Indian Defense

ECO Code: E11

Opening Moves

1. d4 Nf6 2. c4 e6 3. Nf3 Bb4+

Overview

The Bogo-Indian begins 1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 e6 3.Nf3 Bb4+. Variations branch out depending on how White blocks the check.

Strengths

  • Simple to play
  • Strategic and sound
  • Not very theoretical

Weaknesses

  • White may be able to capture two bishops
  • White maintains a space advantage
  • Not very sharp
