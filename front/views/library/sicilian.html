Sicilian Defense

ECO Code: B20–B99

Opening Moves

1. e4 c5

Overview

The Sicilian Defense is the most popular choice of aggressive players with the black pieces. Often White will play 2.Nf3 and 3.d4 which will gain central space, but it allows Black to benefit by exchanging a central pawn for a bishop's pawn.

Strengths

  • Creates unbalanced games
  • Black has good attacking chances
  • An excellent opening to play when you need to win

Weaknesses

  • White has many ways to meet the Sicilian
  • White gets attacking chances in the main variations
  • Lots of theory behind it
