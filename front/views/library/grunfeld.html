Grunfeld Defense

ECO Code: D80-D99

Opening Moves

1. d4 Nf6 2. c4 g6 3. Nc3 d5

Overview

The Grunfeld Defense appears after the moves 1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 g6 3.Nc3 d5.

Strengths

  • Active defense
  • Black only has to knock down, not build up
  • Unbalancing

Weaknesses

  • White's space advanteges
  • White gets a strong center
  • Theoretical opening
